I'm John, a GIS Developer.

About me

I have one year of professional experience in mapping electric utilities for local energy companies and several years studying geography and geospatial science and technology. My degrees are bachelors (Concord University, 2019) and masters (UNCG, 2021) in Geography. Currenly I'm working toward a second masters degree in GIS (MGIS, Penn State U.) focused on GIS automation and web application development. I am skilled in geospatial analysis, GIS web application development, and GIS automation. Versed in cloud and server GIS for solving big data needs, open web mapping for visualization, and database development and management. I am interested in the growth of FOSS solutions in the geospatial industry and have a good foundation with using well-known open source GIS software - I am also very confident in exploring new software as I am introduced. This industry is evolving rapidly and I amm adapting with it. In my student career, I used GIS to assist with research projects in physical geography, I made maps that gave geographic context to my research. I have since transitioned to using GIS as the focus of my work, developing my geospatial analysis and GIS development skillsets to assist companies to optimize business operations.
